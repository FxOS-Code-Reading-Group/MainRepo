00037    * that if no events are processed between 2 notifications (new followed
00038    * by ongoing, or ongoing followed by a new) we want to be as aggresive as
00039    * possible on the clean-up of the memory.  After all, we are trying to
00040    * keep Gecko alive as long as possible.
00041    */
