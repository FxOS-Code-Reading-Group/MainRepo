説明

nsTSubstring is the most abstract class in the string hierarchy. It represents a single contiguous array of characters, which may or may not be null-terminated. This type is not instantiated directly. A sub-class is instantiated instead. For example, see nsTString.

NAMES: nsAString for wide characters nsACString for narrow characters

Many of the accessors on nsTSubstring are inlined as an optimization.
