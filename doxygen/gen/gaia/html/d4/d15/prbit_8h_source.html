00125 /*
00126 ** Macros for rotate left and right. The argument 'a' must be an unsigned
00127 ** 32-bit integer type such as PRUint32.
00128 **
00129 ** There is no rotate operation in the C Language, so the construct
00130 ** (a << 4) | (a >> 28) is frequently used instead. Most compilers convert
00131 ** this to a rotate instruction, but MSVC doesn't without a little help.
00132 ** To get MSVC to generate a rotate instruction, we have to use the _rotl
00133 ** or _rotr intrinsic and use a pragma to make it inline.
00134 **
00135 ** Note: MSVC in VS2005 will do an inline rotate instruction on the above
00136 ** construct.
00137 */

00145 #else
