説明

Used by |gfxFontEntry| to represent the SVG table of an OpenType font. Handles lazy parsing of the SVG documents in the table, looking up SVG glyphs and rendering SVG glyphs. Each |gfxFontEntry| owns at most one |gfxSVGGlyphs| instance.
