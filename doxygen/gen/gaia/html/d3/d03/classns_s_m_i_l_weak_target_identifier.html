Class: nsSMILWeakTargetIdentifier

Version of the above struct that uses non-owning pointers. These are kept private, to ensure that they aren't ever dereferenced (or used at all, outside of Equals()).

This is solely for comparisons to determine if a target has changed from one sample to the next.
