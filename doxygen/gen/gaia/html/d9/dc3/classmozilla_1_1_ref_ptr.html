説明

template<typename T>
class mozilla::RefPtr< T >

RefPtr points to a refcounted thing that has AddRef and Release methods to increase/decrease the refcount, respectively. After a RefPtr<T> is assigned a T*, the T* can be used through the RefPtr as if it were a T*.

A RefPtr can forget its underlying T*, which results in the T* being wrapped in a temporary object until the T* is either re-adopted from or released by the temporary.
