説明

Heap-allocated chunk of arbitrary data with threadsafe refcounting. Typically you would allocate one of these, fill it in, and then treat it as immutable while it's shared. This only guarantees 4-byte alignment of the data. For alignment we simply assume that the refcount is at least 4-byte aligned and its size is divisible by 4.
