説明

An AudioChunk represents a multi-channel buffer of audio samples. It references an underlying ThreadSharedObject which manages the lifetime of the buffer. An AudioChunk maintains its own duration and channel data pointers so it can represent a subinterval of a buffer without copying. An AudioChunk can store its individual channels anywhere; it maintains separate pointers to each channel's buffer.
