説明

Represents a download object.

覚え書き:
This object is no longer updated once it enters a completed state. Completed states are the following: nsIDownloadManager::DOWNLOAD_FINISHED nsIDownloadManager::DOWNLOAD_FAILED nsIDownloadManager::DOWNLOAD_CANCELED nsIDownloadManager::DOWNLOAD_BLOCKED_PARENTAL nsIDownloadManager::DOWNLOAD_DIRTY nsIDownloadManager::DOWNLOAD_BLOCKED_POLICY
