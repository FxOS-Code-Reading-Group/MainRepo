列挙型

enum SVGAutoRenderState::RenderMode
列挙型の値:
NORMAL 

Used to inform SVG frames that they should paint as normal.

CLIP 

Used to inform SVG frames when they are painting as the child of a simple clipPath. In this case they should only draw their basic geometry as a path. They should not fill, stroke, or paint anything else.

CLIP_MASK 

Used to inform SVG frames when they are painting as the child of a complex clipPath that requires the use of a clip mask. In this case they should only draw their basic geometry as a path and then fill it using fully opaque white. They should not stroke, or paint anything else.
