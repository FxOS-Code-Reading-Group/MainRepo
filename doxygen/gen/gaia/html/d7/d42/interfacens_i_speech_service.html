説明

The main interface of a speech synthesis service.

A service's speak method could be implemented in two ways: 1. Indirect audio - the service is responsible for outputting audio. The service calls the nsISpeechTask.dispatch* methods directly. Starting with dispatchStart() and ending with dispatchEnd or dispatchError().

2. Direct audio - the service provides us with PCM-16 data, and we output it. The service does not call the dispatch task methods directly. Instead, audio information is provided at setup(), and audio data is sent with sendAudio(). The utterance is terminated with an empty sendAudio().
