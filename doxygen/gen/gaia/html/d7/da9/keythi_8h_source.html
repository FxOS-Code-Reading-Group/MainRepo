00012 /*
00013 ** RFC 4055 Section 1.2 specifies three different RSA key types.
00014 **
00015 ** rsaKey maps to keys with SEC_OID_PKCS1_RSA_ENCRYPTION and can be used for
00016 ** both encryption and signatures with old (PKCS #1 v1.5) and new (PKCS #1
00017 ** v2.1) padding schemes.
00018 **
00019 ** rsaPssKey maps to keys with SEC_OID_PKCS1_RSA_PSS_SIGNATURE and may only
00020 ** be used for signatures with PSS padding (PKCS #1 v2.1).
00021 **
00022 ** rsaOaepKey maps to keys with SEC_OID_PKCS1_RSA_OAEP_ENCRYPTION and may only
00023 ** be used for encryption with OAEP padding (PKCS #1 v2.1).
00024 */
