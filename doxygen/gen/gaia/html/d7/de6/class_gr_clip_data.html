説明

GrClipData encapsulates the information required to construct the clip masks. 'fOrigin' is only non-zero when saveLayer has been called with an offset bounding box. The clips in 'fClipStack' are in device coordinates (i.e., they have been translated by -fOrigin w.r.t. the canvas' device coordinates).
