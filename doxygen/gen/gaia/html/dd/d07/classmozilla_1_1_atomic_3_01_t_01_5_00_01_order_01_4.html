template<typename T, MemoryOrdering Order>
class mozilla::Atomic< T *, Order >

Atomic<T> implementation for pointer types.

An atomic compare-and-swap primitive for pointer variables is provided, as are atomic increment and decement operators. Also provided are the compound assignment operators for addition and subtraction. Atomic swap (via exchange()) is included as well.
