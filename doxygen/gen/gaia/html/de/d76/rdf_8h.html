The following macros are to aid in vocabulary definition. They creates const char*'s for "kURI[prefix]_[name]", appropriate complete namespace qualification on the URI, e.g.,

define RDF_NAMESPACE_URI "http://www.w3.org/TR/WD-rdf-syntax#" DEFINE_RDF_ELEMENT(RDF_NAMESPACE_URI, RDF, ID);

will define:

kURIRDF_ID to be "http://www.w3.org/TR/WD-rdf-syntax#ID"
