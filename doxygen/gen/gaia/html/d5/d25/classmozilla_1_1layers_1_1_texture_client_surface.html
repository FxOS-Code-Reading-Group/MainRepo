TextureClient is the abstraction that allows us to share data between the content and the compositor side. TextureClient can also provide with some more more "producer" facing APIs such as TextureClientSurface and TextureClientYCbCr, that can be queried using AsTextureCLientSurface(), etc. Interface for TextureClients that can be updated using a gfxASurface.
