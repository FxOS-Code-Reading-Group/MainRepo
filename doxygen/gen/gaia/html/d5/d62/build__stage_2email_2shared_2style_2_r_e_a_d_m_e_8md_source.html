00017 Specific Attributes
00018 -------------------
00019 
00020 A `data-type` attribute is used when the `type` and `role` attributes are not specific enough. Here’s the list of its possible values.
00021 
00022 * `action`: used in `action_menu.css`, title + action selection + accept/dismiss buttons
00023 * `confirm`: used in `confirm.css`, message + accept/dismiss buttons
00024 * `edit`: used in `edit_mode.css`, edition panel with dialog-like button toolbar
00025 * `object`: used in `action_menu.css`, action selection + accept/dismiss buttons
00026 * `switch`: used in `switches.css`, turns a checkbox into an ON/OFF switch
