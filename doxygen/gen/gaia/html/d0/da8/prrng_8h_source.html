00021 /*
00022 ** PR_GetRandomNoise() -- Get random noise from the host platform
00023 **
00024 ** Description:
00025 ** PR_GetRandomNoise() provides, depending on platform, a random value.
00026 ** The length of the random value is dependent on platform and the
00027 ** platform's ability to provide a random value at that moment.
00028 **
00029 ** The intent of PR_GetRandomNoise() is to provide a "seed" value for a
00030 ** another random number generator that may be suitable for
00031 ** cryptographic operations. This implies that the random value
00032 ** provided may not be, by itself, cryptographically secure. The value
00033 ** generated by PR_GetRandomNoise() is at best, extremely difficult to
00034 ** predict and is as non-deterministic as the underlying platfrom can
00035 ** provide.
00036 **
00037 ** Inputs:
00038 **   buf -- pointer to a caller supplied buffer to contain the
00039 **          generated random number. buf must be at least as large as
00040 **          is specified in the 'size' argument.
00041 **
00042 **   size -- the requested size of the generated random number
00043 **
00044 ** Outputs:
00045 **   a random number provided in 'buf'.
00046 **
00047 ** Returns:
00048 **   PRSize value equal to the size of the random number actually
00049 **   generated, or zero. The generated size may be less than the size
00050 **   requested. A return value of zero means that PR_GetRandomNoise() is
00051 **   not implemented on this platform, or there is no available noise
00052 **   available to be returned at the time of the call.
00053 **
00054 ** Restrictions:
00055 **   Calls to PR_GetRandomNoise() may use a lot of CPU on some platforms.
00056 **   Some platforms may block for up to a few seconds while they
00057 **   accumulate some noise. Busy machines generate lots of noise, but
00058 **   care is advised when using PR_GetRandomNoise() frequently in your
00059 **   application.
00060 **
00061 ** History:
00062 **   Parts of the model dependent implementation for PR_GetRandomNoise()
00063 **   were taken in whole or part from code previously in Netscape's NSS
00064 **   component.
00065 **
00066 */
00067 NSPR_API(PRSize) PR_GetRandomNoise( 
00068     void    *buf,
00069     PRSize  size
