#define NS_TRANSFER_CONTRACTID   "@mozilla.org/transfer;1"

A component with this contract ID will be created each time a download is started, and nsITransfer::Init will be called on it and an observer will be set.

Notifications of the download progress will happen via nsIWebProgressListener/nsIWebProgressListener2.

INTERFACES THAT MUST BE IMPLEMENTED: nsITransfer nsIWebProgressListener nsIWebProgressListener2
