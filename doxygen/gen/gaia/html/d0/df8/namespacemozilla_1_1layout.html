enum mozilla::layout::ScrollingBehavior
列挙型の値:
DEFAULT_SCROLLING 

Use default scrolling behavior, which is synchronous: web content is reflowed and repainted for every scroll or zoom.

ASYNC_PAN_ZOOM 

Use asynchronous panning and zooming, in which prerendered content can be translated and scaled independently of the thread painting content, without content reflowing or repainting.
