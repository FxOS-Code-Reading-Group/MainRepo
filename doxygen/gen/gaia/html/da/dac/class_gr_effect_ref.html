class GrEffect

説明

A Wrapper class for GrEffect. Its ref-count will track owners that may use effects to enqueue new draw operations separately from ownership within a deferred drawing queue. When the GrEffectRef ref count reaches zero the scratch GrResources owned by the effect can be recycled in service of later draws. However, the deferred draw queue may still own direct references to the underlying GrEffect.
