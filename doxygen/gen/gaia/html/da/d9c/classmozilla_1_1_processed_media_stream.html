This stream processes zero or more input streams in parallel to produce its output. The details of how the output is produced are handled by subclasses overriding the ProduceOutput method.

This gets called after we've computed the blocking states for all streams (mBlocked is up to date up to mStateComputedTime). Also, we've produced output for all streams up to this one.
